Person to Person Payments

Send gifts or payments — or receive them — with this convenient service.

You don't even need their account details!

After a quick online enrollment in Popmoney®, you'll be able to add contacts and send them money safely and securely -- by text message or e-mail!

It's easy to get started

  • Start by having a U.S. address and a TD Bank personal checking or money market account
  • Proceed through the quick enrollment steps1, providing mobile / e-mail for validation and security
  • Once enrolled, you can add additional contacts to the service any time by providing their delivery info -- whether e-mail or mobile phone (or even bank account info, if desired)

Person-to-Person Payments lets you choose from several delivery options, including next-day delivery.

    Ideal for:

  • Sending a gift to family or friends -- there's even an electronic greeting card feature
  • Paying your landlord or a babysitter
  • Paying back friends for a split dinner check
  • Reminding someone of funds they owe, with the convenient Request Money feature

Your enrollment also includes our external transfers service -- move funds between your TD Bank personal accounts and those you have at other institutions.
